Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app → tap View team plans → scroll to the bottom of the paywall → tap Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored if it is still active on the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Botanica Lashes and tap Cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do Team plans work?

The team owner purchases a Team 3, 5, or 10 plan. The owner's app then generates a team code. Share this code with your team members — they tap Have a team code? in the app to join and gain full Pro access without purchasing their own subscription.

I joined a team but the Pro features are not unlocked

Make sure iCloud is enabled on your device (Settings → [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Drive should be on). If iCloud is off, team features cannot sync. Force-quit and reopen the app after enabling iCloud.

The lash overlay doesn't align correctly

For best results, use a well-lit, front-facing photo with the face clearly visible and centered. Extreme angles or heavy shadows may reduce detection accuracy.
