Privacy Policy

BotanicaLashes · Effective date: June 29, 2026

ANASTASIIA SHILOVSKAIA ("we", "us", or "our") built BotanicaLashes as a paid iOS application. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

1. Photos and Camera

BotanicaLashes accesses your device camera and photo library to apply virtual lash overlays on images you choose. All image processing is performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit for face detection. No photos or images are uploaded to our servers or any third-party server.

2. iCloud Identity (Team Features)

If you use Team features, we store an anonymous iCloud user identifier — provided by Apple — in Apple's CloudKit database. This identifier is used solely to associate you with a team seat. It cannot be used to identify you outside of iCloud. This data is stored in Apple's infrastructure and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Subscription and Purchase Data (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your device identifier and purchase history to process and validate your subscription. For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any directly identifiable personal information.
  • We do not upload or store your photos on any server.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not display third-party advertising.

5. Data Retention

  • Photos: processed in memory only; never stored permanently by us.
  • CloudKit team data: retained until you leave the team or the team is deleted by the owner.
  • Purchase data: retained by RevenueCat as described in their policy.

6. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple CloudKit — team seat synchronisation
  • Google ML Kit — on-device face detection (no data leaves your device)

7. Children's Privacy

BotanicaLashes is not directed to children under 4. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Support page.
